Headers

There are three types of headers. Header, Subheader, and Subsubheader. Each type will get increasingly smaller with each one. Header as a title for a section, subheader as a title for a section inside of a section, and subsubheader as a title for a section inside of a section inside of a section. It gets confusing, so we'll stop at three headers.

Below are the three types of headers/titles for a blog thread.

  • Header

  • Subheader

  • Subsubheader

Images and Links

You can add images to your thread, they will all be the same width (80%). Although the aspect ratio will not change, the image may be cropped.

You can make lists with <ol> or <ul>. Links are also supported with a custom color.
